LOST IN VIVO

HORROR IS IN MY BLOOD

“Lost In Vivo” is an indie horror game that pulls many

influences from the great horror series “Silent Hill”.

This is one of the first games I have ever worked on

and was thrilled that my song “Broken”, inspired by the

works of Akira Yamaoka, was chosen to as the trailer

song for the game! Please check out the “Lost In Vivo”

music tab to hear the songs I contributed to the game!
